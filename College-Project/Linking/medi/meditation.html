meditation and pranayama

RELAXATION AND STRESS RELIEF THROUGH MEDITATION

Meditation is a way to relieve stress at the mental level and help us have a healthy mind, to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state.

PRANAYAMA - THE CONTROL OF BREATH

Pranayama is the practice of breath regulation. It's a main component of yoga, an exercise for physical and mental wellness. Pranayama involves different breathing techniques. You can also practice them while meditating or on their own.
